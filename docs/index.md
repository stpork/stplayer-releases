# 🔹 Privacy Policy (Google Play Ready)
**Last updated:** March 2026

This application ("the App") does not collect personal data directly. However, it uses third-party services and network protocols that may process certain technical information as described below.

## Third-Party Services
The App integrates third-party services (such as Google Firebase) for analytics, crash reporting, and performance monitoring.

These services may automatically collect:

* Device and app information (e.g., device model, OS version, app version)
* Usage and diagnostic data
* Pseudonymous identifiers (e.g., installation IDs)

This data is processed by the respective providers in accordance with their own privacy policies:
[https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

The App developer does not control how these third parties process or retain such data.

## Third-Party Content Providers
When using content provided by external services, the App communicates directly with those providers.

These providers may receive:

* A pseudonymous device identifier
* Basic device information
* Standard network request data

All such data is processed solely by the respective third-party service under its own terms and privacy policy.

If you use BitTorrent-based content sources, your IP address is visible to:

* Torrent trackers
* Other peers in the network

This is an inherent characteristic of the BitTorrent protocol and occurs independently of the App.

## Data Control
The App does not:

* Require user accounts
* Collect names, email addresses, or payment information
* Store personal data on its own servers

## Your Choices
You can limit or prevent data collection by:

* Disabling analytics (where available)
* Avoiding third-party content sources
* Not using BitTorrent-based features
* Uninstalling the App
