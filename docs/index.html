Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Stream Transport Player ("the App") handles user data.

Summary

The App does NOT require user registration and does not ask you to provide your name, email address, phone number, or payment information.

However, when you use the App, certain technical data may be collected, processed, or shared automatically by third-party services integrated into the App, by external content providers you choose to access, or by peer-to-peer network protocols used by specific features.

Data Processed by the App and Third Parties

1. Diagnostics, Analytics, and Performance

The App uses third-party services such as Google Firebase Crashlytics, Firebase Analytics, and Firebase Performance Monitoring.

These services may process technical and diagnostic information, including:

  • device and app information, such as device model, operating system version, app version, and build information
  • app usage events
  • crash reports and error diagnostics
  • performance information
  • pseudonymous identifiers, such as installation identifiers assigned by the provider

This processing is used to:

  • detect and fix crashes and errors
  • monitor app stability and performance
  • understand overall usage patterns and improve the App

These third-party services process data under their own terms and privacy policies, including Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

2. External Content Providers

The App can connect to third-party content providers selected by the user.

When you access such providers through the App, those providers may receive technical data necessary to deliver content or authorize access, such as:

  • pseudonymous device identifiers
  • basic device information
  • network request metadata
  • requests for content, playback, or catalog browsing

If you use peer-to-peer or BitTorrent-based features, your IP address may be visible to:

  • trackers
  • peers participating in the same network

This visibility is inherent to the BitTorrent protocol and is not separately controlled by the App developer.

The App developer does not operate these third-party provider services and is not responsible for their independent privacy practices.

Data handled by external content providers is subject to their own terms and privacy policies.

Data We Do Not Request Directly

The App does NOT directly request or require:

  • account registration
  • your name
  • your email address
  • your phone number
  • your contacts
  • your microphone
  • your camera
  • your precise location
  • payment card information

Data Sharing

Depending on the features you use, technical data may be processed or shared with:

  • Google Firebase services used for analytics, crash reporting, and performance monitoring
  • third-party content providers you choose to access through the App
  • peer-to-peer network participants and trackers when using BitTorrent-based features

The App developer does NOT sell user data to data brokers or advertising networks.

Retention

Data retention is primarily determined by the relevant third-party service provider.

Examples may include:

  • crash and diagnostic data retained for a limited period by analytics or crash-reporting providers
  • analytics data retained according to provider configuration
  • locally stored identifiers remaining on your device until the App is uninstalled or its app data is cleared

Security

The App uses reasonable technical measures to protect data in transit. Communications with supported online services are generally encrypted using HTTPS where provided by the relevant service.

Your Choices

You can limit data processing by:

  • not using optional third-party content sources
  • not using peer-to-peer or BitTorrent-based features
  • restricting analytics where such controls are available on your device or within the App
  • uninstalling the App and clearing app data

EEA / UK Users

If you are located in the EEA or UK, the legal basis for processing may include legitimate interests in maintaining, securing, and improving the App. Where required by applicable law, consent will be requested before optional analytics or similar processing is enabled.
